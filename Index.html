Welcome

Block Party is BACK and BETTER than EVER!! Join us for the most EPIC day of the year with your friends for an experience built JUST FOR YOU and what God has in store for your life!! Whether you've never stepped foot in a church or you've been riding with us forever — it doesn't matter, Block Party is for EVERYONE!! We're splitting into two separate sessions so you get more space, more energy, and an experience made specifically for YOUR grade!!

Get ready for a day LOADED with insane games, amazing food, and music that will touch your soul in more ways than one — and it all wraps up with a message that will actually hit different!! Because at the end of the day, we believe God has something REAL to say to YOUR generation — and Block Party is the place where you'll hear it!! Whether you have questions about faith, life, or just who you are — God sees you, knows you, and has a plan for you!!
